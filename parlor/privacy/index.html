Sixer

Privacy Policy

Last reviewed: July 5, 2026

Sixer stores gameplay state locally on your device, including active puzzle progress, completed-puzzle history, streaks, achievements, preferences, and Premium entitlement state. Sixer does not require an account and does not ask you to provide a name, email address, contacts, photos, location, or other personal profile information.

Sixer uses Sentry to collect crash reports in release builds. Crash reports can include technical diagnostics such as the stack trace, app version, device model, operating system version, performance timing, and diagnostic information needed to diagnose reliability issues. They are not intended to include puzzle contents or account information.

Sixer shows ads to non-Premium users through Google AdMob, using the Google Mobile Ads SDK. To serve, measure, and protect ads, Google and its partners may process device and usage information such as advertising identifiers, device characteristics, approximate location inferred by the ad SDK, ad interactions, and app activity. Sixer Premium is a one-time in-app purchase that removes ads.

Purchases are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store. Sixer receives purchase status needed to unlock Premium, but it does not receive your full payment card details.

You can remove local gameplay data by uninstalling the app. For questions about privacy, crash reports, or purchase state, use the support email listed with Sixer on the App Store or Google Play.

Ad consent (EEA, UK & Switzerland)

Where required, Sixer shows a Google-certified consent message (via Google's User Messaging Platform) before serving personalized ads, and records your choice. If you decline, or consent is unavailable, ads are limited to non-personalized ads. On iOS, Sixer also respects the system App Tracking Transparency setting; if you do not allow tracking, the advertising identifier is not used to personalize ads. You can change these choices later in your device settings.

Children's privacy

Sixer is a general-audience app and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). Sixer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad requests are tagged as not child-directed and limited to family-friendly (PG-rated) ad content. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us using the support link below and we will address it.

Your privacy rights (GDPR & CCPA/CPRA)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your information, to object to processing, and to withdraw ad consent. California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and to opt out of its “sale” or “sharing” for cross-context behavioral advertising. Because Sixer has no account system, most data is stored only on your device and is removed when you uninstall; buying Premium removes ads entirely. To exercise any right or ask a privacy question, use the support email below.

Privacy Policy

Kingfisher: Cozy Bird Puzzle · Effective 2026-07-18

HomePrivacySupport

Data stored on your device

Kingfisher: Cozy Bird Puzzle stores gameplay state locally on your device, including active game progress, completed-game history, statistics, preferences, reminder and ad-frequency state, and entitlement state. The app does not require a developer account. This data is not uploaded as gameplay data and remains until you clear app storage or uninstall the app, subject to operating-system backup and restore behavior.

Crash reporting

Production releases use Firebase Crashlytics. Crash reports may include stack traces, app version, device model, operating system information, device state, timing, installation identifiers, and other technical diagnostics needed to fix reliability problems. Debug and automated capture builds keep collection disabled.

Analytics

Kingfisher: Cozy Bird Puzzle uses Firebase Analytics to measure app usage and improve the game. The provider may collect app-instance and device information, launches, sessions, technical diagnostics, and app-interaction events. Limited game events cover tutorial completion and puzzle starts and completions. The app does not send user IDs, user-entered text, puzzle contents, filenames, or advertising IDs as custom analytics parameters.

Advertising

Kingfisher: Cozy Bird Puzzle may show ads through Google AdMob. To serve, measure, and protect ads, Google and its partners may process information such as advertising identifiers, device characteristics, approximate location inferred by the ad SDK, ad interactions, and app activity. The app does not request location permission. Where required, Google User Messaging Platform requests or manages consent before ads; a privacy-options entry appears in Settings when Google requires it.

Premium purchases and verification

Store purchases are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. The developer does not receive your full payment-card details.

When you purchase or restore Premium, the app sends its package name, product ID, store purchase token, purchase or order identifier when available, transaction date, billing source, and restore flag to the developer's HTTPS purchase-verification service. The service uses this information to verify entitlement with the store, prevent token replay and fraud, restore access, and respond to refunds or revocations. Records are retained only as reasonably necessary for those purposes, support, security, and legal obligations; they are not used for advertising or sold.

Optional local notifications

The app can schedule an optional local reminder. Notification permission is requested only when you enable it. The app reads the device timezone locally and may reschedule after a reboot or update; it does not upload the timezone, register a remote push token, or send marketing notifications.

Support communications

If you email support, the developer receives your email address, message, and anything you voluntarily include. It is used to answer the request, diagnose problems, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations, then retained only as reasonably necessary for those purposes.

Sharing, retention, and deletion

The developer does not sell personal information. Information may be handled by the providers described above for ads, analytics, crash reporting, commerce, purchase verification, and hosting. You can remove local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling, subject to device backup and restore. Because the app has no developer-hosted account, there is no server account to delete.

To ask about or request deletion of support, purchase-verification, or provider data associated with you, contact support@greenbean.studio. Records required for transactions, disputes, security, fraud prevention, or law may be retained.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. It should not be listed as child-directed unless its implementation, advertising configuration, and store declarations are changed to meet applicable requirements.
